Welcome to Our Flask CRUD Application

Our Flask CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) application is a powerful tool for managing your data with ease. Built using the Flask framework and MySQL database, our application offers a user-friendly interface for performing various operations on your records.

Key Features:

  1. Record Management: Add, view, update, and delete records seamlessly.
  2. Data Generation: Generate and insert multiple records quickly with our data generator tool.
  3. Interactive Dashboards: Visualize your data with dynamic bar charts, pie charts, histograms, and scatter plots.
  4. Responsive Design: Enjoy a responsive design that works seamlessly across desktop and mobile devices.
  5. Easy Integration: Easily integrate our application into your existing projects with our modular and customizable codebase.

Whether you're a developer, data analyst, or business owner, our Flask CRUD application empowers you to efficiently manage your data and gain valuable insights.
